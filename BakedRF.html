Baked Asian Rockfish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a shallow baking pan with aluminum foil
  2. In a small bowl combine sesame oil, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, ginger, garlic, and pepper flakes.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 10 to 15 minutes.
